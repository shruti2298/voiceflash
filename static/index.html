🧠 Memory Card Voice Bot

Listen up. Repeat it back. Don't blink. 🎧

🎙️ Type your name and hit Start voice game. Allow microphone access when your browser asks — that's the bot listening, nothing is recorded or saved.

🤖 The host greets you and speaks a short sequence of words out loud, e.g. "Repeat after me: apple, tiger, river." Just listen — don't respond yet.

🗣️ Say the words back in the same order, then stop talking. The bot patiently waits until you're actually finished before judging you — try talking over it mid-sentence too, it'll stop cleanly and let you go.

🏆 Nail it → you score points and the next sequence is one word longer. Miss it → game over, final score locked in and added to the leaderboard below.
